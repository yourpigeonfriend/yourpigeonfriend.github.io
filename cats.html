In Loving Memory of the I-80 Road Cats

Every winter, I drive from Chicago, IL to Aurora, CO with my pet parrot. This year, I noticed an unusual amount of domestic cats dead on the side of the road. They were always in pairs on opposite sides. I found this to be both heartbreaking and romantic. I like to think that they were trying to meet in the middle...
