Memories Shared Between Me and a Magpie / 2023

Memories Shared Between Me and a Magpie is an artist book centered around the personal growth that I have experienced in my first year of adulthood. A small risograph book sits snugly in the magpie's back, holding images taken during recent emotionally meaningful moments.
