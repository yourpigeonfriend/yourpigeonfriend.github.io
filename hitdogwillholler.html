A Hit Dog Will Holler

Seven mallard duck decoys displayed on a cherry shelf. Slipcast and glazed ceramic.

This piece was displayed in School of the Art Institute of Chicago's "New Work" show in July 2024.

An altered version of this piece was displayed in Gallery 1922 for me and Ada Hamilton's "Permanent Pasture" show.
