Mesa / 2022

Mesa is a ceramic sculpture of a thick-billed parrot. These parrots once lived in the Southern United States, but have been pushed out and are now facing extinction. Habitat loss, climate change, the illegal pet trade, and poor wildlife management have left these birds spiraling downward... Mesa is a frozen moment in time for this dying species. She is grounded, on her back, struggling, and looking her oppressor in the eyes. Mesa opens up the conversation around a lack of empathy toward the Earth's neglected and dying avian species through her romantic, dramatic, and suspended final moments of life.
