Avian Instruments 01-03 / 2023

Chelsea (Avian Instrument 01)

Chelsea is my first attempt at making a touch-based ceramic instrument. She contains a Ford Focus tweeter and a 555 timer oscillator.

Quincy (Avian Instrument 03)

Quincy is my third "Avian Instrument." He's a Crackle-Grackle... I used a 709 op amp and the STEIM cracklebox/kraakdoos schematic with 6 touch points on Quincy's back. The speaker is a Ford tweeter.
